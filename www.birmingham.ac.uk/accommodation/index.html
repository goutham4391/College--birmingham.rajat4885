Student Accommodation

Coming to Birmingham to study might be your first time living away from home. Our student accommodation will allow you to enjoy your independence in safe, welcoming and sociable surroundings.

Your guide to accommodation for September 2022

Still looking for your perfect room?

We still have accommodation available across our villages. Simply give us a call on 0121 512 2158 (Monday – Friday 09:00 – 17:00) to join our vibrant student community. 

Accommodation applications are now open

Whether you're an offer holder starting in September 2022 or a current student looking to call off the house hunt, we want you to be part of our student community.

Oakley Court

Oakley Court comprises 21 individual purpose-built flats set around an attractive landscaped courtyard and accommodates 213 students.

  • Shared Bathroom
  • Self-catered
  • £146 per week

Ashcroft

Ashcroft is set in the heart of leafy Pritchatts Park in landscaped grounds, near to the Metchley sports pitches.

  • Shared Bathroom
  • Self-catered
  • £107 - £119 per week

Bournbrook

Home to 178 students living in stylish en-suite accommodation, Bournbrook is a modern residential development that opened its doors in 2017.

  • En-suite
  • Self-catered
  • £179 per week

Jarratt Hall

Built around a residents' courtyard, Jarratt Hall is home to more than 700 undergraduate and postgraduate students, all living in en-suite flats.

  • En-suite
  • Self-catered
  • £165 per week

Shackleton (Shared)

Housing 350 students at the heart of the Vale Village community, Shackleton offers mainly catered accommodation through the Meal Plan.

  • Shared Bathroom
  • Meal Plan
  • £172 - £175 per week
