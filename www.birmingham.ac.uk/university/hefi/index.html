If you haven't worked with HEFi before watch this short video introduction to find out more.

 

 

About HEFi

HEFi's mission is to support staff to deliver innovative and inclusive research-intensive teaching. The aim is to optimise student learning and prepare students for their futures.

HEFi is open-access to facilitate shared learning with and from colleagues across the higher education sector.
