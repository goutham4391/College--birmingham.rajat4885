Innovating with business to deliver impact across research and education

Our team of business-focused experts work across all sectors, geographies and business sizes. From supporting student placements and graduate employment to helping business get involved in our innovations to achieve their own goals. We are here for collaboration across R&D and your business strategy. We also identify and licence out our own intellectual property, set up spinout companies, and provide academic consultancy services.

We also provide leading CPD with Degree Apprenticeships, leadership qualifications and a strong focus on digital skills for business and the public sector, working with all organisations to reduce skills shortages and skills gaps across the region and beyond.
